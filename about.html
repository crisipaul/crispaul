My story

I am a freelance writer based in Pembrokeshire, Wales. Previously of London, and Argentina. Writer is an ambiguous noun. Its flexibility serves me well. I write for performance, late modern poetry, journalism, and copy writing. My work features in the Welsh, UK, and Argentinian national press. Including those big hitters the BBC and The Guardian. I used to be on the payroll of Associated Newspapers- before I left them. I've written search engine optimised copy for some of the most successful global brands, and stacks of small businesses. I have an obsessive interest in the world- broadly left wing- think arts, politics, economics, and travel. I can create or I can hack. Open to commissions.


On the voluntary side of things I am a school speaker for Amnesty Interntional, human rights activist, and I run creative writing workshops for Big Issue Cymru vendors. I am on the board of Poetry Wales magazine.

Services

I make money by writing articles, press releases, and search engine optimised copy. I know enough CSS HTML mark-up to be useful, and know my way round social media. I make art for money and for kicks too. Poetry, music, sound art, and performance art. Good night Derrida. Good morning something new...
